## SDL2
Event / Polling Based

- Dualshock 4
- DualSense
- Xbox 360 (XInput / sdl2 generic gamepad)
- Xbox One
- Switch Pro
- Joy-Con Pair
- Left Joy-Con
- Right Joy-Con
- GameCube
- Generic Joystick

Features: Gyro, Accelerometer, Rumble, HD Rumble, LED, Player Number, Touch Points, Adaptive Triggers, Microphone, Speaker, Cursor, IR
